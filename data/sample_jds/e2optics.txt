Regional Quality Manager
E2 Optics — Remote

E2 Optics is a technology integrator specializing in data center infrastructure.

Responsibilities:
- Define and standardize QA/QC procedures for structured cabling, OSP, AV, DAS, and data center systems
- Oversee jobsite inspections, audits, and closeout documentation
- Ensure work meets NEC, BICSI, TIA, and customer-specific standards
- Lead cross-functional quality programs across field operations
- Support onboarding and certification programs including BICSI and client-specific requirements
- Establish and monitor KPIs for quality performance
- Travel to regional data center construction sites as required

Requirements:
- 5+ years quality management in low voltage, structured cabling, or data center construction
- BICSI RCDD, PMP, or equivalent certifications preferred
- Knowledge of BICSI standards, ANSI/TIA codes, and electrical/communications best practices
- Experience with NEC, BICSI, TIA compliance and jobsite inspection
- Building and construction knowledge for data center environments
